Rooms

It is time to fall in love with the Bellingham Hotel, all over again. Experience newly renovated rooms as of July 4, 2016 and find out why Bellingham Hotel continues to set the standards of luxury in downtown Bellingham. For more information and a sneak peek of the new rooms, click here.

Elegance surrounds you as you enter our guest rooms and suites at the Bellingham Hotel, Bellingham. Our 450 hotel rooms feature lavish bedding, luxurious amenities and conveniences such as high-speed wireless internet access, a feature that is complimentary for our Bellingham President's Club members when staying in our Bellingham luxury accommodations.

Rooms range from Bellingham and Deluxe rooms, to our Executive Suites that offer partial privacy to separate the bedroom from the living area. Spacious bathrooms feature marble vanities and adjacent dressing area.

The hotel has two signature suites with multiple bedrooms and dining area. For more information and virtual tours visit our Guest Room and Suites sections for descriptions of the different types of, whether it be a specialty room or a luxurious suite.

A variety of ADA rooms are available at the hotel, when inquiring on availability of the hotels ADA rooms, please email reservations@Bellingham.com or call the hotel directly at 8686 3838 38383 for more assistance.
